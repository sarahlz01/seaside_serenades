Indie

While Indie music isn't a genre on its own, it's a parent genre that includes indie rock, indie punk, and more. Indie music, by definition, is independently released music. One featured artist is Colleen Dow, whose music can be classified as indie-punk.

EDM

EDM, or electronic dance music, is another parent genre, which includes disco and synth-pop. Its origin can be traced back to Jamaica in the 1960s.
One featured artist is Franco Aurelio. Learn more about him in our artist spotlights page!

Dance Pop

Dance Pop is a broad term that encompasses the genres of pop, dance music, and more. It's a subgenre of pop that originated in the 1970s and '80s, and is known for its catchy and danceable beats. One featured artist is Franco Aurelio. Learn more about him in our artist spotlights page!

Hip Hop

While hip hop is often used interchangeably with rap, it's a broader culture. Originating in New York in the 1970s, it led to the introduction of some cultural cornerstones, like grafitti and break dancing. One featured artist is Franco Aurelio. Learn more about him in our artist spotlights page!

Jazz

Jazz is a genre that originated in the African American communities of New Orleans, Louisiana, in the late 19th century. The genre is classified by its complex chords and improvization.

Lo-fi

The term lo-fi comes from low-fidelity, which essentially references a low-quality or stripped-back production. It's a mix of several different genres, including jazz and hip hop, and is perfect for relaxing or as a background when working.
Lo-fi features in most of our mood playlists.
