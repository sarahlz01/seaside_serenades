Artist Spotlights 🎙️

Read about some of our favorite artists!

Audrey - She/Her

An independent bedroom pop artist and a lover of Taylor Swift. Her most popular song is "Chai Tea" and she recently released a new single called "novelty". Connect with her on Instagram at @audreygaytan.

Elizabeth Wong - She/Her

A "profressional overthinker" with her most popular song being "Kid I Used to Be." which discusses the hardships of maturing. Her Instagram is @elizabethwongggg.

Nxdia - They/He/She

Pronounced "Nadia", Nxdia was born in Cairo, Egypt and loves her khalto fatma's cooking. They enjoy documenting things and want to build a friendly community around their music. His most popular song is "OUCH!" and his instagram is @nxdiamusic.

Franco Aurelio - He/Him

His music is a unique blend of hip-hop, EDM, trap, and dance-pop. He is a Catholic who is passionate about leading others on a moral path. His instagram is @thefrancoradio.
